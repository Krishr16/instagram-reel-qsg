## Posting a Reel

Instagram provides three entry points for opening the Reel composer: the Create button, the Reels tab, and your profile page. Each entry point opens the same Reel composer, where you choose between two content sources: recording a new video, or uploading an existing file from your gallery. The next three pages describe the three entry points. The two pages after that describe the two content sources. The final page in this chapter describes the shared sequence for publishing the finished Reel.

### Prerequisites

Before you post a Reel, confirm the following requirements.

- An active Instagram account. Business and Creator accounts unlock additional settings, such as product tagging.
- The latest version of the Instagram app, installed on your iOS or Android device.
- A video file, a set of photos, or both, stored on your device.
- A video file in MP4 or MOV format, at 1080 x 1920 resolution, in a 9:16 aspect ratio.
- Camera roll permission, granted to the Instagram app.

### Post a Reel from the Create button

The Create button, at the top of the home screen, is the fastest entry point to the Reel composer from any screen in the app.

To open the Reel composer from the Create button, use the following sequence.

1. At the top of the home screen, select the **plus (+)** icon.
2. From the menu, select **Reel**.

### Post a Reel from the Reels tab

The Reels tab opens the Reel composer directly from the dedicated Reels stream, without a return to the home screen.

To open the Reel composer from the Reels tab, use the following sequence.

1. On the bottom navigation bar, select the **Reels** icon.
2. At the top of the Reels tab, select the **camera** icon.

***[Insert screenshot: Reels tab with the camera icon highlighted]***

The Reel composer opens. Continue to [Record a new Reel](record-a-new-reel.md) to record a new video, or to [Upload a Reel from your gallery](upload-a-reel-from-your-gallery.md) to upload an existing file.

> **Additional entry point:** The Stories camera also opens the same Reel composer. From the home screen, swipe right to open the Stories camera, then swipe to the **Reel** mode at the bottom of the screen.

---
### Post a Reel from your profile

Your profile page opens the Reel composer while you review your existing content grid.

To open the Reel composer from your profile, use the following sequence.

1. On the bottom navigation bar, select your **profile picture**.
2. On your profile page, select the **plus (+)** icon.
3. From the menu, select **Reel**.

***[Insert screenshot: Profile page with the plus icon highlighted]***

The Reel composer opens. Continue to [Record a new Reel](record-a-new-reel.md) to record a new video, or to [Upload a Reel from your gallery](upload-a-reel-from-your-gallery.md) to upload an existing file.
