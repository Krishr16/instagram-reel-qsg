## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Cause | Resolution |
|---|---|---|
| The Reel appears blurry after upload. | The source file resolution is below the recommended value. | Export the video at 1080 x 1920 resolution, in MP4 format, using the H.264 codec. |
| The selected music track does not play. | The track license does not cover your account region or account type. | Select a different track from the Instagram audio library, or use original audio instead. |
| The Reel does not appear in the Reels tab. | The **Share to Feed** setting is set to off, or the Reel length exceeds 3 minutes. | Check the Reel settings on the [Publish the Reel](publish-the-reel.md) page, and reduce the Reel length to 3 minutes or less. |
| The cover text is not legible on the profile grid. | The cover frame places text near the top or bottom edge of the image. | Select a cover frame or image with text positioned away from the top and bottom edges. |
| The tag option is unavailable for a file. | Instagram does not support tagging on a video inside a carousel post. | Publish the video as a single-file Reel instead of as a carousel post. |
| The Templates option does not appear. | The source Reel does not include music, or the source Reel contains fewer than 3 clips. | Choose a different Reel that includes music and at least 3 clips as the template source. |
