## Best practices

- Recording or exporting video at 1080 x 1920 resolution, in a 9:16 aspect ratio, to prevent cropping.
- Keeping the Reel length at 3 minutes or less, to qualify for recommendation to non-followers.
- Placing on-screen text away from the top 15 percent and bottom 15 percent of the frame, to avoid overlap with the Instagram interface.
- Selecting a cover frame with legible text before you select Share.
- Using licensed audio from the Instagram audio library, to avoid a muted track after publication.
- Entering a caption that contains descriptive keywords, to support discovery in Instagram search results.
- Adding alternative text to a Reel posted from the web, to support accessibility.
