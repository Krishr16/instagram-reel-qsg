### Upload a Reel from your gallery

Upload an existing video or photos when the content is already stored on your device. Complete this task after you open the Reel composer from [Post a Reel from the Create button](post-a-reel-from-the-create-button.md), [Post a Reel from the Reels tab](post-a-reel-from-the-reels-tab.md), or [Post a Reel from your profile](post-a-reel-from-your-profile.md).

To upload a Reel from your gallery, use the following sequence.

1. In the bottom-left corner of the Reel composer, select the **gallery icon**.
   
![Record or upload](../instagram-reel-qsg/images/record-upload.png)

1. Select one video file, or select photos to create a photo-based Reel.
2. For a photo-based Reel, select a photo, then drag the duration handle to set its display time.
3. In the top-right corner, select the **checkmark icon** to confirm your selection.

The selected content loads into the Reel composer. Continue to [Publish the Reel](publish-the-reel.md) to publish the Reel.
