# Platform reference: Mobile app and web browser

Instagram supports two platforms for posting a Reel: the mobile app and Instagram on the web. The mobile app supports every entry point described in [Posting a Reel](posting-a-reel.md) and every content source described in [Record a new Reel](record-a-new-reel.md) and [Upload a Reel from your gallery](upload-a-reel-from-your-gallery.md). Instagram on the web supports only the upload of an existing video file.

## Mobile app

- Opening the Reel composer from the Create button, the Reels tab, your profile page, or the Stories camera.
- Recording a new video in the app, uploading an existing video or photo set from your gallery, using a template, or using Remix.
- Trimming clips, reordering clips, adding music from the Instagram audio library, adding on-screen text, and adding stickers.
- Setting the Share to Feed toggle and the Show on Profile Grid toggle.

## Instagram on the web

To post a Reel on Instagram on the web, use the following sequence.

1. In a web browser, go to instagram.com and sign in.
2. In the bottom-left corner, click your profile picture, then click **Create**.
3. In the dialog box, click **Post**.
4. Click **Select from computer**, then select one video file.

   > **Note:** A single video file publishes as a Reel. An upload of two or more media files publishes as a carousel post instead of a Reel.

5. In the top-right corner, click **Next**.
6. Below **Cover photo**, drag the slider to select a frame from the video, or click **Select from computer** to upload a separate cover image.
7. Below **Trim**, drag the slider handles to trim the beginning or the end of the clip.
8. In the top-right corner, click **Next**.
9. In the caption field, enter a caption.
10. In the **Location** field, enter a location, if applicable.
11. Click **Tag People** to tag other accounts, if applicable.
12. Click **Accessibility** to add alternative text for the video, if applicable.
13. In the top-right corner, click **Share**.
